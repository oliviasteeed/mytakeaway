My Takeaway is a commonplace website.

What is it?

This is a place for me to collect the poignant parts I get from books (and other media) so I don't immediately forget the life-changing breakthroughs I am always forgetting. Maybe if I have all the good stuff in one place it'll last.

Why is it?

Why not? Reading is fun, but remembering what you read is more fun, coding is alright and design is bliss.
